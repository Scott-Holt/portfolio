About Me

I'm currently a freelance front end web developer with a solid grasp of the fundementals and a sharp focus on semantic, responsive design.

Being a self taught web developer has not been an easy path to put it lightly. The challenge of learning the languages in addition to the isolation made it feel like a dead end road on some days. But what got me through some of my hardest days was reaching out to the coding community. It really showed me how benneficial it is to talk to other like minded people, and also made me that much more eager to start working with a team. I never really knew how passionate I was about learning and pushing my self until developing became apart of my life.

With about a decade worth of freelancing experience in the audio engineering field, I have become proficeient in the delicate process of translating vision into reality, which is a skill that has transferred elegantly into my web development career.
